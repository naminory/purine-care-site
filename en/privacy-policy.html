Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PurineCare (hereinafter "the App") handles personal information.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects and uses the following information.

Meal Record Data

Meal data entered by the user (food name, portion size, purine content, calories, protein, fat, carbohydrates) and water intake records. This data is stored in the on-device database and is not transmitted to external servers.

Photo Data (AI Meal Recognition)

Photos of meals taken or selected when using the AI meal recognition feature. Photo data is sent to Google Gemini API provided by Google LLC for image analysis. Transmitted image data is not stored on the App's servers.

Meal Data (AI Advice)

The AI advice feature sends recorded meal data (food name, portion size in grams, purine content, water intake) to Google Gemini API to generate dietary improvement advice.

Health Data

When the user grants sync permission, the App reads and writes water intake and nutrition data (calories, protein, fat, carbohydrates) with Apple Health.

2. Purpose of Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes.

  • Storing meal records and displaying statistics
  • Automatic food identification via AI meal recognition (photos sent to Google Gemini API)
  • Generating dietary advice via AI (meal data sent to Google Gemini API)
  • Data synchronization with Apple Health

3. Third-Party Disclosure

The App uses the following third-party service.

Google Gemini API (Provided by Google LLC)

  • Data sent: Meal photos, meal record data (food name, portion size, purine content, water intake)
  • Purpose: Automatic food identification, dietary advice generation
  • Data handling: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Consent: User consent for data transmission is obtained upon first use of AI features

Other than the above, the App does not provide users' personal information to third parties.

4. User Consent

When using AI features (meal recognition and advice), consent for data transmission is requested upon first use. If you do not consent, AI features will be unavailable; however, all other basic features (manual meal recording, statistics, water management, etc.) remain fully available.

5. Data Storage

All meal records and settings data are stored locally on the user's device. No account registration is required, and the App does not store data on its own servers.

6. Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App will delete all local data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be changed without prior notice. Users will be notified within the App of any significant changes.
