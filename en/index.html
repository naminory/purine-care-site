PurineCare

Purine Intake Management App

About the App

PurineCare is a purine intake management app designed for people concerned about gout and hyperuricemia.

  • Easily record purine and calorie intake from a database of over 400 foods
  • AI automatically recognizes foods from meal photos
  • AI-powered dietary advice based on your daily meal data
  • Track and manage water intake
  • Weekly statistics graphs to understand intake trends
  • Apple Health integration
